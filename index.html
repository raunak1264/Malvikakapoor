Contemporary Artist

Malvika Kapoor

A passionate contemporary artist and dedicated art educator with a profound commitment to nurturing creativity and artistic excellence. With formal education in Fine Arts from one of India's most prestigious institutions, I bring both traditional wisdom and contemporary vision to every canvas and classroom.

My artistic journey is rooted in the belief that art is not just about creating beauty—it's about expressing emotions, telling stories, and connecting souls. Through my work and teaching, I strive to inspire individuals to discover their unique creative voice.

"Art is the language of the soul. Every brushstroke is a word, every color is an emotion, and every canvas tells a story waiting to be discovered."

BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts) – Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi
MFA (Master of Fine Arts) – Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi
Professional Contemporary Artist – Exhibitions & Art Shows
Founder – Malvika Kapoor Art Gallery and Classes, Lucknow
