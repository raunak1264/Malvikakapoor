Contemporary Artist & Art Educator

Malvika Kapoor

A contemporary artist and art educator with over 25 years of practice, trained in Fine Arts at Banaras Hindu University. My work has been exhibited across India, including solo exhibitions at Jehangir Art Gallery, Mumbai, and through my association with the Lalit Kala Akademi.

Painting has been at the centre of my life for more than two decades. Through my artwork and teaching, I encourage people to observe closely, think creatively, and find their own visual language. This space brings together my work, my teaching, and my continuing journey as an artist.

"Art is the language of the soul. Every brushstroke is a word, every color is an emotion, and every canvas tells a story waiting to be discovered."
