Contemporary Artist & Founder

Malvika Kapoor

A passionate contemporary artist and dedicated art educator with over 25 years of creative practice. With formal education in Fine Arts from one of India's most prestigious institutions, I bring both traditional wisdom and a contemporary vision to every canvas and classroom.

My artistic journey is rooted in the belief that art is not just about creating beauty — it's about expressing emotions, telling stories, and connecting souls. Through my work and teaching, I strive to inspire individuals to discover their unique creative voice.

"Art is the language of the soul. Every brushstroke is a word, every color is an emotion, and every canvas tells a story waiting to be discovered."
